CO-CURRICULAR AND EXTRA-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES:

Education being the all-round development of the innate talents of a child, we give as many opportunities as possible to every student to participate in a variety of co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. Participation in these activities will not only help to draw-out and develop his/her various talents and aptitudes but also inculcate in him/her a spirit of healthy competition. It will also teach him/her the values of cooperation and sportsmanship, making him/her a more disciplined individual. To achieve these aims, we have provided facilities for indoor and outdoor games, athletics, literary and cultural activities

.

LITERARY ACTIVITIES:

Debate, Elocution, Essay Writing, Extempore, etc. in English, Hindi and Telugu. Quiz and General Knowledge competitions are an integral part of them.

CULTURAL ACTIVITIES:

Singing, Music, Dance and Rangoli.

CLUBS:

Science, Maths, Music, Social Service, Gk and Literary.

ASSOCIATIONS:

Games & Sports and Literary Association.
